Why Graphical Password Authentication (GPA)

The world has seen a changing trend in the way we approach passwords, the well-known and often used alphanumeric passwords are usually preferred to be quite long and complicated, making it difficult for an average user to remember and keep track of, in addition, security issues like shoulder surfing, dictionary attacks and brute force methods are still cause for concern in today's virtual space. Graphical Password Authenticators come into the picture offering human friendly alternatives to alphanumeric passwords that are easy to remember, all while offering better security than it's counterparts. To showcase what this would look like in a real world scenario, we at Team Pineapple Pizza are proud to present our very own GPA.
